Mileage Reimbursement Form

As an employee of CIS 115 Software you will be reimbursed for work-related travel as follows:

Fuel and use of car $0.54 per mile
Lunch when daily mileage exceeds 80 miles $10.50 per day
Dinner, Breakfast and Hotel for each night of travel $150.00 per night

You must pay for expenses above the approved amounts. Be sure to keep a detailed record of your dates of travel, mileage, and overnight stays.
